The Perfect PB&J

Description

A peanut butter and jelly sandwich is very easy to make, is delicious and filling. I love making one for breakfast, when I'm in a rush or as a midnight snack.

Ingredients

  1. Two slices of bread (whichever is preferable
  2. Favorite jar of jelly
  3. jar of creamy peanut butter
  4. A Butter knife or something to spread
  5. A knife
  6. A cutting Board
  7. Toaster (if toasted bread is preferred)
  8. A plate

Directions

  • Toast bread if preferred, otherwise lay bread on the cutting board.
  • With the butter knife, scoop up peanut butter and spread it across one of the piece of bread.
  • On the other piece of bread, scoop up and spread jelly.
  • Place bith two halves/sliced of bread together, with the jelly on top.
  • With the Knife, cut the sandwich into triagles.
  • Place the sandwich on the plate.
  • Enjoy!
